Shiv Sharma was born in Simla, India and had his early education in Delhi and Bombay. He took his Master’s degree in philosophy from Punjab University and worked as a journalist in Bombay for about two years. He writes in English as well as Urdu, a language he studied up to his Bachelor’s degree level.
Shiv Sharma takes part regularly in poetry readings and “mushairas” in the United Kingdom. His ghazals are being increasingly sought by singers cutting or planning to cut their own CDs and he was an area finalist in an Urdu poetry competition organised by Channel 4.
