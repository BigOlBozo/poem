Meaning of the Poems
With her viral poem Rape Joke, Patricia Lockwood has reinvented how we talk about rape. She has casually reawakened a generation’s interest in poetry. (If this is poetry, who wouldn’t want to read it more often?) And she may well be the first person with an actual sense of humour to write an attack on rape jokes. Or is it actually a defence of rape jokes? Ah, you see, that’s why it’s so clever. This poem which has gone viral , is an oblique mini-masterpiece.
